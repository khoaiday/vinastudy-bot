### Issue: AI refuses to generate character 9 (Hồ Tinh)

### Issue: Math symbols not appearing
**Solution:** Explicitly ask AI to "ensure floating math symbols are prominent and visible"

### Issue: Wrong art style (too photorealistic)
**Solution:** Explicitly reference "Kingdom Rush 2D art style" and show examples

### Issue: Characters don't match color palette
**Solution:** Specify RGB/hex codes: "Use red #FF5555" etc.

### Issue: Transparent backgrounds not working
**Solution:** 
- Specify "transparent background (PNG)" in prompt
- If AI can't deliver, use background removal tool (remove.bg)
- Export as PNG with transparency
