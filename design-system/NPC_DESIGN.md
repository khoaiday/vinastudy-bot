# NPC CHARACTER DESIGN
**VInaStudy Bot — Design System v6**

## 1. THẦY LONG — Math Teacher (Teacher NPC)

### 👨‍🏫 Character Overview
- **Role:** Math teacher, mentor figure, quest giver, lesson guide
- **Purpose:** Appears in classroom scenes, dialog cutscenes, tutorial sequences, quest briefings
- **Personality:** Wise, approachable, encouraging, patient with students
- **Relationship:** Respected mentor, protective, believes in student potential

---

### 📋 Visual Specifications

#### **Age & Appearance**
- **Age:** ~45-55, mature, wise-looking
- **Ethnicity:** Vietnamese (Southeast Asian features)
- **Build:** Average build, upright posture (teacher stance)
- **Facial Features:**
  - Warm brown eyes with kind expression
  - Slight smile or encouraging smile
  - May have light gray streaks in hair/beard (shows wisdom/experience)
  - Gentle but dignified expression

#### **Clothing Style**
- **Primary Attire:** Traditional Vietnamese teacher outfit
- **Áo Thụng:** Long traditional robe in warm colors (cream, light brown, or soft indigo)
- **Sash:** Vietnamese silk sash around waist (warm colors: #C8960C gold or #B87333 copper)
- **Accessories:**
  - Pair of reading glasses hanging on neck (optional, can wear on head)
  - Wooden ruler or teaching stick in hand (symbol of teacher)
  - Old worn leather satchel with books/scrolls
  - Wrist prayer beads or traditional Buddhist string (cultural touch)
- **Footwear:** Simple cloth shoes or sandals (traditional Vietnamese)

#### **Color Palette**
| Element | Color | Hex | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----|-------|
| Áo Thụng Primary | Cream/Ivory | #F5EED6 | Warm, approachable |
| Áo Thụng Secondary | Light Brown | #8B6F47 | Earthy, scholarly |
| Sash | Gold/Copper | #C8960C | Wisdom accent |
| Accent Trim | Brown | #5C4033 | Pattern borders |
| Hair | Black/Gray | #333 or #666 | Mix of black with gray |
| Skin | Warm Brown | #C9A876 | Southeast Asian |

#### **Props & Accessories**
- **Teaching Tool:** Wooden ruler, bamboo stick, or pointing stick (symbol of education)
- **Satchel:** Worn leather bag filled with scrolls, books, writing materials
- **Scroll/Books:** Ancient Vietnamese texts (Lĩnh Nam Chích Quái references, math scrolls)
- **Optional:** Meditation beads, amulet, or traditional pendant
- **Writing Utensil:** Brush or traditional Vietnamese calligraphy pen

#### **Pose & Expression**
- **Default Pose:** Standing upright with encouraging gesture, hand extended outward (welcoming)
- **Alternative Poses:**
  - Sitting at desk with teaching materials
  - Pointing at board/scroll with ruler
  - Hands clasped together in warm greeting
- **Facial Expression:** Warm smile, kind eyes, slightly raised eyebrows (engaged, encouraging)
- **Overall Vibe:** Approachable wisdom, encouraging mentor energy, trustworthy

---

### 🎨 Art Style & Rendering

**Thầy Long follows the SAME art style as all playable characters:**
- Kingdom Rush game card illustration style
- 2D stylized digital painting (NOT photorealistic)
- Bright illustrated card aesthetic suitable for ages 8-10
- Vietnamese historical fantasy with cultural authenticity
- Inspired by Vietnamese sơn mài (lacquerware) art
- Bold outlines, rich jewel-tone colors

**Key Difference from Battle Characters:**
- More peaceful, scholarly demeanor instead of combat-ready
- Warm golden/indoor lighting (classroom or study) instead of dramatic battle lighting
- Approachable and gentle expression instead of heroic/powerful
- Academic environment elements (scrolls, desk, board) instead of weapons/armor

### 💬 Dialog & Interactions

**Thầy Long serves multiple functions:**
1. **Tutorial Guide** - Teaches game mechanics, math concepts
2. **Quest Giver** - Assigns math challenges and learning quests
3. **Encouragement** - Provides positive feedback and motivation
4. **Story Elements** - Explains lore, Vietnamese cultural context

**Sample Dialog:)**
- "Chào em! Hôm nay chúng ta sẽ học về số học..."
- "Rất tốt! Em đã giải đúng bài toán. Hãy tiếp tục cố gắng!"
- "Các con số này đang bị Hồ Tinh giam giữ. Hãy giành lại chúng!"
- "Em là học sinh thông minh. Anh tin em sẽ thành công!"

---

### 🎭 Character Archetype

**Mentor Figure / Wise Teacher**
- Inspired by: Vietnamese educators, Buddhist monks/scholars, Confucian teachers
- Cultural Element: Represents knowledge, wisdom, cultural continuity
- Player Relationship: Supportive guide, motivator, authority figure
